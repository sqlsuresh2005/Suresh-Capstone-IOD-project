Pree notes :

The capstone project will allow you to showcase skills learned throughout the course by defining, designing and delivering an end-to-end data science solution.
Students must prepare a 12-minute PowerPoint presentation and a 5-minute Jupyter Notebook technical presentation. This presentation will occur on the last day of the course.

The PowerPoint presentation should cover the following:

Business Problem
Data Science Problem
Stakeholders
Dataset
EDA
Models
Evaluation and comparison
Result
Conclusion / Future Work
The Jupyter Notebook should be readable and well-commented

Deliverables:

1. Powerpoint slide
2. Jupyter notebook
3. Raw Data
4. Project Documentation
